<p><p>Displacement measurement sensors are vital for precise distance gauging in various applications. Laser distance measurement sensors use laser beams for high accuracy and long-range measurement, while ultrasonic sensors emit sound waves to determine distance based on echo return time. LED distance sensors utilize light for shorter measurements, and photoelectric sensors detect changes in light intensity for object detection and distance gauging. Other types may include capacitive or inductive sensors, catering to specific industrial requirements. Each type serves unique environments and precision needs.</p></p>
