<p><p>The Non-contact Liquid Handling Workstation market features several key technologies. Acoustic technology uses sound waves to precisely dispense liquids without physical contact. Immediate drop on demand technology allows for rapid and precise droplet dispensing based on user commands. Positive pressure technology utilizes air pressure to eject liquid droplets, ensuring accuracy and minimizing contamination risks. Droplet verification technology monitors and confirms the presence and size of droplets dispensed, enhancing reliability and precision in liquid handling applications.</p></p>
