<p><p>The patient warming blanket market is segmented into three main types: full body, lower body, and upper body blankets. Full body warming blankets provide comprehensive thermal coverage, ensuring overall temperature regulation during medical procedures. Lower body blankets focus on warming just the legs and feet, often used in specific surgical contexts where only this area requires heating. Upper body blankets target the chest and arms, beneficial for surgeries or treatments involving the upper body, enhancing patient comfort and reducing hypothermia risk.</p></p>
