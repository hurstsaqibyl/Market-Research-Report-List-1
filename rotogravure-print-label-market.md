<p><p>The Rotogravure Print Label Market encompasses various types of label applications. Pressure-sensitive labels use adhesive that activates on contact, offering versatility and easy application. Glue-applied labels are adhered using liquid adhesives, often providing a stronger bond. Heat-shrink labels shrink to fit the product upon heating, ideal for achieving a snug fit on irregular shapes. Stretch sleeve labels are elastic and can easily conform to the contours of containers, providing high-quality graphics and 360-degree branding. Each type serves distinct packaging needs.</p></p>
