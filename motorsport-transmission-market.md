<p><ul><li>H-Gearbox</li><li>Sequential Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorsport transmission market primarily consists of two types: H-gearbox and sequential gearbox systems. H-gearboxes, commonly found in traditional vehicles, utilize a pattern resembling the letter "H" for shifting gears, providing a tactile and familiar driving experience. In contrast, sequential gearboxes allow for faster gear changes by engaging gears in a linear sequence, often through paddle shifters. Both systems cater to different racing styles, with H-gearboxes favoring vintage enthusiasts, while sequential gearboxes are preferred for high-performance racing scenarios.</p></p>
