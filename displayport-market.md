<p><p>The DisplayPort market includes several types catering to different applications. Embedded DisplayPort (eDP) is used in laptops and monitors for high-resolution displays. Wireless DisplayPort enables wireless transmission of video and audio signals. Internal DisplayPort is integrated into devices, while Micro DisplayPort offers a smaller connector for portable devices. MyDP/SlimPort allows connections to HDMI and VGA, supporting mobile devices. Mini DisplayPort is a compact version popular in laptops and tablets, providing versatility in connectivity options.</p></p>
