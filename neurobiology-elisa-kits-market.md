<p><p>The Neurobiology ELISA Kits market features several types: Direct ELISA, which detects antigens using a labeled antibody; Sandwich ELISA, where antibodies capture the target antigen between two layers; Indirect ELISA, which measures the presence of antibodies against an antigen; and Competitive ELISA, which assesses the concentration of an antigen by competition between sample and labeled antigen. Each type offers unique advantages for quantifying proteins and biomarkers relevant to neurobiological research, driving demand in clinical and research settings.</p></p>
