<p><p>The optical fruit sorting machinery market is categorized into several types based on the mechanism used for sorting. </p><p>Roller-type machines utilize rollers to gently move fruits through a detection zone where images are analyzed for quality and size. Belt-type sorting relies on conveyor belts to transport fruits while cameras scan for imperfections. Chute-type systems involve fruits being funneled down a chute, allowing gravity to assist in sorting. Other types may include specialized designs tailored for specific fruit varieties or unique sorting requirements.</p></p>
