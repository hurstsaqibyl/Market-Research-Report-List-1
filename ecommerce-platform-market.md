<p><p>Ecommerce platforms are categorized into two main markets: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based platforms are hosted remotely, allowing businesses to access and manage their online stores from anywhere with internet connectivity. They offer scalability, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premise solutions require businesses to install and maintain the software on their own servers, providing greater control and customization but often involving higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance responsibilities. Each type suits different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
