<p><p>The Aggregate Concrete market can be categorized into two main types: Natural Materials Aggregate Concrete and By-Products Aggregate Concrete. Natural Materials Aggregate Concrete utilizes naturally occurring materials such as gravel, sand, and crushed stone, which provide strength and durability to concrete mixes. In contrast, By-Products Aggregate Concrete is derived from industrial or post-consumer waste materials, such as recycled concrete or slag, contributing to sustainable construction practices. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and environmental sustainability of concrete.</p></p>
