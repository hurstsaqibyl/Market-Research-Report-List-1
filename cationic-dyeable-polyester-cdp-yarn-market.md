<p><p>Cationic Dyeable Polyester (CDP) yarns are primarily categorized into DTY (Draw Textured Yarn) and FDY (Fully Drawn Yarn). DTY yarn is known for its textured, soft feel and is used in applications like knitwear and woven fabrics, offering excellent dye affinity. FDY yarn, on the other hand, is smooth and has higher tensile strength, making it suitable for applications requiring durability. Additionally, the "Other" category includes specialty yarns that exhibit unique properties for niche textile applications.</p></p>
