<p><ul><li>Solid State Lidar</li><li>Mechanical/Scanning Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lidar mapping encompasses two primary market types: solid-state Lidar and mechanical/scanning Lidar. Solid-state Lidar utilizes fixed arrays of laser emitters and detectors, providing a compact design with enhanced durability and cost-effectiveness, ideal for automotive and drone applications. In contrast, mechanical/scanning Lidar involves rotating components that sweep lasers across the environment, delivering high-resolution data suitable for complex terrain analysis and urban modeling. Both technologies contribute significantly to applications in autonomous vehicles, surveying, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
