<p><p>The Angiography System market encompasses various types, including Head, Chest, Abdominal, and Heart Angiography. Head angiography focuses on vascular imaging of the brain, aiding in diagnosing conditions like aneurysms and strokes. Chest angiography evaluates blood vessels in the lungs and heart, crucial for identifying coronary artery diseases. Abdominal angiography visualizes organs and blood vessels in the abdomen, assisting in diagnosing conditions like gastrointestinal bleeding. Heart angiography, or coronary angiography, specifically examines coronary arteries to assess heart health and diagnose other cardiac issues.</p></p>
