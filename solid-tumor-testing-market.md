<p><p>The solid tumor testing market is segmented into conventional and non-conventional testing. Conventional testing typically involves histopathology and imaging techniques like CT and MRI, focusing on tumor morphology and staging. In contrast, non-conventional testing includes advanced methodologies such as liquid biopsies, genetic profiling, and molecular diagnostics that analyze tumor-specific biomarkers and genomic alterations. These innovative approaches enhance precision medicine, allowing for personalized treatment strategies and improved patient outcomes while providing deeper insights into tumor biology.</p></p>
