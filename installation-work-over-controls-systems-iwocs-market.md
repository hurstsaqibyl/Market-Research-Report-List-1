<p><p>Installation Work Over Control Systems (IWOCS) are essential for subsea operations, facilitating interventions and maintenance of underwater equipment. The market consists of various types: </p><p>1. **Direct Type** operates systems directly using hydraulic control.</p><p>2. **Piloted Type** employs smaller pilot systems to control larger hydraulic operations.</p><p>3. **Electrohydraulic Type** integrates electrical controls with hydraulic systems for improved precision.</p><p>4. **Electrohydraulic Multiplexed Type** optimizes control through multiple channels, enhancing efficiency and reducing equipment footprint. Each type caters to specific operational needs in the offshore industry.</p></p>
