<p><strong>The Dehydrated Food Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Dried</li><li>Freeze Dried</li><li>Vacuum Dried</li><li>Sun Dried</li><li>Hot Air Dried</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dehydrated food market includes several types, each with distinct methods and characteristics. Spray drying involves atomizing food into fine droplets, creating lightweight powders. Freeze drying removes moisture via sublimation, preserving flavor and nutrients. Vacuum drying reduces moisture at low temperatures under vacuum conditions, while sun drying relies on natural sunlight for hydration removal, often resulting in preserved textures. Hot air drying circulates heated air to evaporate moisture efficiently. Other methods may include osmotic dehydration and microwave drying, offering diverse options for preservation.</p></p>
