<p><p>The Sinuscope endoscopes market comprises two main types: optical fiber endoscopy and electronic endoscopes. Optical fiber endoscopy utilizes flexible optical fibers for illuminating and capturing images of the sinus cavities, offering clear visualization in minimally invasive procedures. In contrast, electronic endoscopes incorporate advanced imaging technology, providing real-time video output with enhanced clarity and precision. Both types are crucial for diagnosing and treating sinus conditions, each offering unique advantages in terms of flexibility, clarity, and ease of use for healthcare professionals.</p></p>
