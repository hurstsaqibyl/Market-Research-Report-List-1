<p><p>The forklift extensions market primarily features two types: roller extensions and box extensions. In contrast, box extensions are designed for carrying flat, box-like items, offering a stable platform for various goods. Both extension types improve forklift versatility, allowing operators to efficiently manage different loads, ultimately boosting productivity and safety in warehousing and logistics operations.</p></p>
