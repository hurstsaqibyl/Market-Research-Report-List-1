<p><ul><li>Chemical Hydrolysis</li><li>Physics Extraction Method</li><li>Enzymolysis Process</li><li>Biological Fermentation Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seaweed fertilizer market encompasses various production methods, each leveraging unique processes. Chemical hydrolysis involves breaking down seaweed using chemical reactions to extract nutrients. The physics extraction method relies on physical techniques, such as pressing or heating, to obtain fertilizer components. Enzymolysis uses enzymes to facilitate the breakdown of seaweed into bioavailable nutrients. Lastly, the biological fermentation process utilizes microorganisms to decompose seaweed, enriching it with beneficial compounds. Together, these methods cater to diverse agricultural needs and promote sustainable practices.</p></p>
