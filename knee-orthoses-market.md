<p><p>The knee orthoses market comprises two main types: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to provide movement support and stabilization, allowing for mobility while assisting with knee function during rehabilitation or activity. In contrast, static orthotics offer rigid support and immobilization to protect the knee from further injury, often used post-surgery or for severe conditions. Both types play crucial roles in managing knee-related issues, enhancing patient recovery, and improving overall joint health.</p></p>
