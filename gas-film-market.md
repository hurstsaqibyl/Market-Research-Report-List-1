<p><ul><li>Porous Membrane</li><li>Homogeneous Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas film market includes two primary types: porous membranes and homogeneous membranes. Porous membranes feature interconnected voids that allow selective gas permeation based on size or solubility, making them ideal for applications like gas separation and filtration. In contrast, homogeneous membranes are uniform in structure, providing consistent gas transport characteristics, often utilized in processes requiring high purity and efficiency. Both types cater to industrial needs, ensuring optimal gas separation and filtration solutions in diverse applications.</p></p>
