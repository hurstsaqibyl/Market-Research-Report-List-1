<p><p>The deepwater manifolds market primarily consists of four types: </p><p>1. **Template Manifold**: A fixed structure installed on the seafloor facilitating multiple well connections to a pipeline. </p><p>2. **Cluster Manifold**: Engages multiple wells in a compact area, optimizing space and production efficiency. </p><p>3. **Pipeline End Manifold (PLEM)**: Positioned at the end of a pipeline, it connects the pipeline to subsea structures. </p><p>4. **Other**: This includes specialized or customized manifolds catering to unique operational requirements or environments.</p></p>
