<p><p>A rotor concentrator is an air pollution control device that utilizes a rotating element to concentrate volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and other pollutants from industrial air streams. It works by adsorbing contaminants onto a rotor and then desorbing them for efficient recovery and treatment. This technology is becoming increasingly vital in industries such as automotive, chemical manufacturing, and food processing, where strict emissions regulations necessitate effective air quality management.</p><p>The Rotor Concentrator Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.9% during the forecast period, driven by the rising awareness of environmental sustainability and regulatory pressures.
