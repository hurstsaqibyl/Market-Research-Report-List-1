<p><p>The pallet truck market is primarily categorized into manual and electric pallet trucks. Manual pallet trucks are operated by manpower, featuring a simple design that allows users to lift and move pallets using a pump handle. They are cost-effective and ideal for smaller operations. In contrast, electric pallet trucks are powered by batteries, enabling easier and faster movement of heavier loads over longer distances. They enhance efficiency and reduce physical strain on operators, making them suitable for larger warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
