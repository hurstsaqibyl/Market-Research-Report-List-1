<p><p>The brass compression fitting market encompasses various components, each serving distinct purposes in plumbing and piping systems. "Union" fittings allow easy disconnection of pipes, facilitating maintenance. "Union Elbow" fittings provide angled connections, ideal for changing pipe direction. "Union TEE" fittings enable branch connections, allowing for the division of flow in multiple directions. "Union Cross" fittings permit connections among four pipes, enhancing system versatility. Together, these components offer robust solutions for fluid transportation in residential and industrial applications.</p></p>
