<p><p>The HVAC air quality sensors market includes various types designed to monitor specific indoor air pollutants and conditions. CO2 sensors primarily measure carbon dioxide levels to assess ventilation adequacy. CO2 and VOC sensors detect both carbon dioxide and volatile organic compounds, crucial for identifying air quality issues. CO2 and temperature sensors monitor carbon dioxide levels alongside temperature for comfort. CO2, humidity, and temperature sensors provide a comprehensive assessment of the indoor environment. VOC sensors focus solely on harmful organic compounds, while particulate matter sensors track airborne particles, contributing to holistic air quality management.</p></p>
