<p><p>The Residual Current Detector (RCD) market includes several types: </p><p>Type AC RCDs detect alternating current leakage, primarily for standard AC circuits. Type A RCDs can sense both AC and pulsating DC leakage currents, suitable for modern appliances. Type B RCDs handle smooth DC currents, often used in photovoltaic systems. Type F RCDs are designed for circuits with mixed loads, including frequency converters. Other RCDs may cater to specific applications or features, adding diversity to the market and addressing varying safety needs.</p></p>
