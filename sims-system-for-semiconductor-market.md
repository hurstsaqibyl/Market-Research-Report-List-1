<p><p>SIMS (Secondary Ion Mass Spectrometry) is a crucial analytical technique utilized in the semiconductor market, primarily divided into ToF (Time-of-Flight) SIMS and Dynamic SIMS. ToF SIMS excels in high spatial resolution and mass sensitivity, making it ideal for surface analysis and thin-film characterizations. In contrast, Dynamic SIMS focuses on bulk material analysis with higher sensitivity and depth profiling capabilities, allowing for the assessment of elemental distributions and composition changes throughout semiconductor layers, essential for process development and quality control.</p></p>
