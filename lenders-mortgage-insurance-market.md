<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Lenders Mortgage Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Borrower-paid (BPMI)</li><li>Lender-paid (LPMI)</li><li>Single Premium</li><li>Split Premium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI) protects lenders against borrower defaults on loans with low down payments. In Borrower-paid PMI (BPMI), borrowers cover the insurance premiums, adding to their monthly payments. Lender-paid PMI (LPMI) involves the lender covering the insurance cost, typically resulting in a higher interest rate for the borrower. Single Premium LMI is a one-time upfront payment made at closing, while Split Premium LMI combines both upfront and monthly payments, offering flexibility to borrowers in managing insurance costs.</p></p>
