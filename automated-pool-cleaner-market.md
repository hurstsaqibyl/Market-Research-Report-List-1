<p><strong>The Automated Pool Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crawler Drive</li><li>Wheel Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automated pool cleaner market features two primary types: crawler drive and wheel drive cleaners. Crawler drive cleaners use tracks to navigate, providing superior maneuverability and climbing ability on pool surfaces, making them ideal for uneven or textured areas. In contrast, wheel drive cleaners utilize wheels for transportation, offering faster movement across flat surfaces. Both types effectively remove debris and dirt from pools, though their performance may vary based on pool shape and surface type, influencing consumer choice.</p></p>
