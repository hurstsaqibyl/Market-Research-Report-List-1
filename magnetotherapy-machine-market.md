<p><strong>The Magnetotherapy Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostatic Therapy</li><li>Dynamic Magnetic Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetotherapy machine market is primarily divided into two types: magnetostatic therapy and dynamic magnetic therapy. Magnetostatic therapy uses static magnetic fields to promote healing, reduce pain, and enhance circulation by applying constant magnetic forces on targeted areas. In contrast, dynamic magnetic therapy involves the use of varying magnetic fields, often through pulsed electromagnetic fields, which can influence cellular processes and improve tissue repair. Both methods are utilized in pain management and rehabilitation, offering distinct therapeutic benefits.</p></p>
