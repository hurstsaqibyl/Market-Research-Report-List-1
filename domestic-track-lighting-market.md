<p><ul><li>Halogen Track Lighting</li><li>LED Track Lighting</li><li>Incandescent Track Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The domestic track lighting market features three primary types: halogen, LED, and incandescent. Halogen track lighting offers bright, focused illumination with a warm hue, ideal for accent lighting but generates heat and consumes more energy. LED track lighting is energy-efficient, has a long lifespan, and provides versatile color options, making it increasingly popular for modern homes. Incandescent track lighting delivers a soft, warm light with simple installation but is less energy-efficient compared to the other options, leading to higher energy costs.</p></p>
