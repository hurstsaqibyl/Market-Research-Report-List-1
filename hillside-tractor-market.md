<p><p>Hillside tractor markets are categorized into two main types: wheeled and crawler tractors. Wheeled tractors offer greater agility and speed, making them ideal for flat terrains and diverse agricultural tasks. In contrast, crawler tractors, equipped with tracks instead of wheels, provide enhanced stability and traction on steep or uneven landscapes, making them suitable for hillside farming. Each type serves specific needs in agriculture, with wheeled tractors excelling in maneuverability and crawlers focusing on durability in challenging conditions.</p></p>
