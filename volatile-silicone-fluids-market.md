<p><p>The volatile silicone fluids market is categorized based on viscosity levels, influencing their applications and performance. Fluids with viscosity below 2 centistokes (cs) are highly mobile, ideal for applications requiring rapid evaporation, such as in personal care products. Those in the 2-5 cs range offer moderate viscosity, suitable for cosmetics and lubricants. Fluids with viscosities between 5-10 cs provide greater thickness, making them useful in industrial applications and formulations needing enhanced stability. The "Others" category includes specialized silicones tailored for unique uses.</p></p>
