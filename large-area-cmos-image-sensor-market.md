<p><strong>The Large Area CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-Illuminated</li><li>Back-Illuminated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Large Area CMOS Image Sensor market can be segmented into Front-Illuminated and Back-Illuminated types. Front-Illuminated sensors have a structure where light enters from the front, resulting in lower sensitivity and efficiency due to obstructions from circuit elements. In contrast, Back-Illuminated sensors have a design that allows light to hit the active region directly from the back, improving light sensitivity and image quality. This distinction influences their applications in fields such as surveillance, medical imaging, and industrial automation.</p></p>
