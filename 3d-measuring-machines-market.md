<p><p>The 3D measuring machines market comprises several types, notably bridge type, gantry type, and cantilever type. </p><p>Bridge type machines feature a stationary bridge over a movable table, ideal for high precision in small to medium-sized components. Gantry type machines have a large structure that moves around the workpiece, suitable for large parts and heavy materials. Cantilever type machines are characterized by a single support arm, offering flexibility in space usage but limited in size capacity. Additionally, other variations may include portable and optical 3D measuring systems.</p></p>
