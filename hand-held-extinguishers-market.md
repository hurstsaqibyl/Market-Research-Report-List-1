<p><p>The hand-held extinguishers market includes several types, each designed for specific fire hazards. Dry Chemical extinguishers are versatile, effective against multiple fire classes, and use a powder to smother flames. Foam extinguishers are ideal for flammable liquid fires, creating a barrier between the fuel and flames. Carbon Dioxide (CO2) extinguishers are effective for electrical fires and liquid fires, displacing oxygen without leaving residue, making them suitable for sensitive equipment. Each type serves crucial roles in fire safety, catering to distinct environments and fire risks.</p></p>
